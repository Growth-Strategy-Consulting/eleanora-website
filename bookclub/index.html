what it is

Not a feed. A room.

Somewhere along the way we started reading alone, fast, for the finish. This is the opposite of that. A slow year of books that ask the big questions, read beside people who are also asking.

the rhythm

How a month goes

i
We pick up the book.
Some months an easy read you'll finish in a weekend. Some months a deeper one worth the whole month. A few, we go all the way through together.
ii
We read it on our own time.
No quizzes, no pressure. Underline the lines that catch you. Bring the ones that stayed.
iii
Then we gather.
Once a month we meet on Zoom, a real round table, and talk about what the book did to us. Ten of us, everyone in it, no audience. Better than reading alone.
