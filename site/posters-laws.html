For your eyes, dreamer

There is a manual to becoming part of the 1%. This is it.

and you were hand-picked to find it

Twelve laws and one threshold, the whole manual to the one percent, only here it wears paint and a frame. Each piece keeps a law the chosen live by and the crowd never bothers to learn. Start at the door. Choose the one that will not let you look away, and let the rest of your life rearrange itself around it. You were not born to play the jester in someone else's court, dreamer. The crown was always yours. This is where you reach for it.

Twelve laws. One is already yours.

Each law keeps its own room, and in every room the art that holds it. The piece on show turns with the season, so the wall is never the same wall twice. Read the law, drift through the pieces, and take the one that was always going to find you.
