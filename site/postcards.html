Postcards

a little light, mailed

A real postcard, in a real mailbox, in a world that stopped sending them.

Not yet

These are coming in September, and they are being built properly rather than fast.

I could have rushed these out cheap and let a printer mail them for me. That version wasn't worth making, so this one is being built by hand instead.

  • Write it to your future self and choose the date it lands.
  • Or send it to the person who needs the reminder more than you do.
  • The story on the back. The place on the front.
  • Sold in sets, priced so sending a stack costs less than sending one.
