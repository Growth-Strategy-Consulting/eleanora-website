Who I take

I don't work with everyone.
I won't pretend otherwise.

This only works if you're actually ready. So before anything else, read this honestly. If the right-hand column is you, we're going to save each other a lot of time.

This is for you if

  • You're genuinely at the end of the rope with the life you built, not just curious or bored.
  • You'll do the work between our calls instead of waiting for me to do it for you. (Newsflash: I won't do it for you. I'm busy.)
  • You can own your part without a running list of who's to blame.
  • You can invest at this level without wrecking yourself to do it.

This is not for you if

  • You want to be fixed without lifting a finger.
  • You want somewhere to vent, not somewhere to change.
  • You want a quick fix and the word "year" already lost you.
  • You're in real crisis right now. You deserve a licensed therapist, and I'll say so. That's care, not a sale.

None of that is me playing hard to get. My time is genuinely limited, because it's one to one. Every seat I give to the wrong person is one I can't give to the right one.

How it works

You don't buy this. You apply for it.

  1. Apply

    A short, honest application. About you, not your budget. Where you are, what you're most done with, why now.

  2. The consult

    A 30-minute call, just us. I get to know where you really are and start building the plan that fits you. A paid seat, not a free pitch.

  3. I decide

    I only take you on if it's a yes for both of us and a spot is open. If it's not, I'll tell you straight, and point you somewhere better.

  4. We begin

    I send you the plan and what it costs, we set the rhythm, and the work starts.

And after

You graduate. You don't disappear.

From day one you're in the community, and you stay there long after the calls end. It's where the wins land, where the friendships happen, where you reach out the day something finally clicks. The deep work has an ending. The room doesn't.

Before you ask

The real questions.

What if I'm not sure I'm ready?
Then the call is exactly for that. Being unsure is human. Wanting to change but being scared of it is not the same as not being ready. We'll figure out which one you are, together, before anything's decided.
Is this therapy?
No. But some days it'll feel like it. The difference is I'm not here to collect a paycheck and nod along. I'm here to tell you the hard truths, the ones you've been paying someone else not to. If you don't want to hear them, keep paying your therapist to hold your hand. And if what you're carrying genuinely needs a clinician, I'll tell you that straight and point you to one. That's the one thing I won't bully you out of.
How long is this?
Depends which door you take. One month to start, learn the method, and get through Aries. Three months for the real, intensive work. And a longer path if you earn it. Quick doesn't hold, so I don't do weekend fixes, but you don't have to sign your life away to begin either.
What does it cost?
I don't put it on this page, because what you actually need isn't one-size. I build a plan around your life and tell you the number on our call. Payment plans exist. And if the price is the first thing you're worried about, no worries. When you're ready to invest in yourself, I'll be here.
What if you don't take me?
Then I'll be honest about why, and point you toward what would actually serve you, whether that's the self-guided work, the community, or something outside my world entirely. A no from me is still meant to help.
